Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This is a fall time favorite! For those pumpkin lovers this cookie recipre is for you. My wife makes these every year and they're fantastic!

Ingredients

  • 2 cup (4 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 cup pumpkin purée
  • 2 large eggs
  • 4 tsp. pure vanilla extract
  • 4 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp. baking soda
  • 2 tsp. pumpkin pie spice
  • 1 tsp. kosher salt
  • 4 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, pumpkin spice, and salt.
  2. In a large bowl using a hand mixer, cream together butter and sugars until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in pumpkin, egg, and vanilla extract until combined, then add flour mixture.
  4. Beat on low until no raw flour appears, then fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Refrigerate dough for 30 minutes.
  6. Preheat the oven to 375° and line two large baking sheets with parchment paper.
  7. Scoop 1” balls onto prepared baking sheets, 2 inches apart, and bake until puffed up and golden around edges, about 12 minutes.

Enjoy!
